About

I received my Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Wuhan University of Technology and was recommended for admission to pursue a Master’s degree at the School of Computer Science and Technology of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I am currently a Master’s student supervised by Prof. Zhang Rui and Prof. Yixin Su from the INTREBID Lab . My current research interests lie in Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), with a focus on extending these technologies to broader real-world applications. Beyond research, I enjoy playing table tennis, badminton, and exploring movies and music in my free time.
If you are interested in my work or our lab’s research, feel free to reach out via luanyun2836 [at] hust.edu.cn.
